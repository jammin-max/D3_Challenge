Correlations Discovered Between Smoking and Age

The percentage of smokers seems to be clustered in the states with a median age of 36-40. A interesting discovery is all, or at least part of the sates that have the highest percentage of smokers are on central daylight time (One outlyer is amongst us).

Four(KY,AR, TN, LA) of the top five states with highest percentage of smokers(above 23%) fall in this bracket. West Virginia with a median age of 42, on Eastern daylight time leads the way.

Jamin Hershberger 2021
